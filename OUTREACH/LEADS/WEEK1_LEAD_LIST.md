## **How to DM them (The "No-BS" CEO Strategy)**

When reaching out to these CEOs, use the following rules to maximize response rates:

1. **Be Direct**: Don't ask "How are you?". Start with the value proposition.
2. **Use "In-Group" Language**: Mention their specific repository or a recent technical problem they solved (e.g., "Ollama's local runtime performance").
3. **The "Gift" First**: Don't ask for a meeting. Offer the **Executive Summary** (the audit) as a gift.
4. **Low Friction**: End with a question that only requires a "Yes" or "No" (e.g., "Would you be open to seeing the results?").

### **Template for LinkedIn/DM:**

**Subject: Audit: Automating [Company Name]'s Architectural Guardrails**

> "Hi [Lead Name],
>
> I've been following [Company Name] and noticed the high PR velocity on your [Repository] repo. 
>
> I'm building **Zaxion**, and we're solving the 'Senior Maintainer Bottleneck' by turning your architectural invariants into **Autonomous Guardrails**.
>
> I've already run a **'Governance Audit'** on your recent PRs and identified where Zaxion would have pre-emptively blocked architectural drift and saved your team hours of manual review.
>
> Would you be open to seeing the Executive Summary I put together?
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]"
